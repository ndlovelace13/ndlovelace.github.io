My Iron Skin

  • Genre: Co-op Survival Horror
  • Engine: Unity 3D
  • Project date: March - April 2023
  • Project URL: https://qrane.itch.io/my-iron-skin

Collect spare parts scattered throughout abandoned locales to escape the horrors hidden within

My Iron Skin is a survival horror experience constructed by a group of five for a Game Certificate Class. Initially a small-scale project for a game jam, this title eventually grew in scope to incorporate co-op and multiple maps. The project heavily focused on constructing a tense, immersive atmosphere, with every portion of the project contributing to that goal. As a gameplay and level designer on this project, I constructed environments that aimed to engross the player in their fear while also being balanced from a gameplay perspective. Every room has at least two ways to exit, enabling the player to escape from the aggressive enemies with enough forethought. Collaborating on a small team for this project allowed me to understand how my role contributed to the whole of the project. It also gave me plenty of experience on how to best communicate my progress and hangups to other members focused on completely different tasks.
